DIGP SOUTH ZONE KARACHI

ABOUT US

Zonal Profile

  • Syed Asad Raza (PSP)

    DIGP South Zone

    With the grace of Allah, I am honored to assume the Charge as The Deputy Inspector General of Police, South Zone Karachi on Sep, 08-2023. My compliments to all the Police Officials, Officers, and Staff. The South Zone Police is committed to professional excellence in providing public safety services. The South Zone Police is very professional in the form of Police Patrol, Crime Investigations, Prevention of Crimes, and Community Policing.

  • Muhammad Shahid, SPS

    DSP/legal, South Zone Karachi

    Legal Branch, South Zone is always a bridge between Police Department and Judiciary. All the matters pertain to Honorable Supreme Court, High Court, MCTC Court, ATC Courts, Lower Courts, NAB Courts Services Tribunal normally deals with this branch. Legal Branch is consisting upon 1. Assembly Question Cell, 2. National Judicial Policy, 3. Summons, Warrants, Notices, 4. MIT Applications of High Court, 5. HRC Supreme Court, 6. CPs, Civil Suits, Criminal Miscellaneous Application, 7. Missing Persons Cases, 8. Judgement Assessment, 9. Suo Moto Matters/ Supreme Court, 10. Service Appeal etc.

  • Insp Muhammad Taufiq

    Reader to DIGP South Zone

    As the Reader Branch Officer at DIGP South Zone Karachi, key responsibilities include overseeing crime statistics maintenance, facilitating the transfer of crime investigations, managing the issuance of rewards to police personnel, monitoring the performance of investigation officers, and conducting formal and informal inspections of South Zone Police Stations and affiliated offices. Additionally, the role involves initiating inquiries into instances of poor performance among police investigation officers. This position requires meticulous attention to detail, effective communication skills, and a commitment to upholding the standards of law enforcement within the South Zone.

  • ASI Syed Mohsin Raza

    Reader Investigation

    The Reader Investigation is child branch of Reader to DIGP South Zone Karachi. As the Reader Investigation my responsibility is facilitating the transfer of crime investigations, managing the issuance of rewards to police personnel, monitoring the performance of investigation officers. Additionally, the role involves initiating inquiries into instances of poor performance among police investigation officers.

  • ASI Umar Hayat Khan Lodhi

    Incharge Statical Branch

    The Statistical Branch is child branch of Reader to DIGP South Zone Karachi. As the head of the Statistical Branch at DIGP South Zone Karachi, my role is centred on the comprehensive management and analysis of crime-related data. I am responsible for overseeing the collection and interpretation of crime statistics, conducting in-depth analysis to identify patterns and trends, and presenting detailed crime reports to inform strategic decision-making. My focus extends to formulating future strategies aimed at reducing crime rates and enhancing public safety. Leveraging advanced analytical tools such as Power BI and Looker Studio etc, my branch ensures a cutting-edge approach to understanding and addressing criminal activities. Additionally, I play a crucial role in identifying crime hotspots, allowing for targeted and proactive law enforcement efforts within the South Zone.

  • ASI Mudassir Amanat

    Accountant

    As the Account Branch Officer at DIGP South Zone Office, responsibilities include meticulous management of police employee payrolls, timely issuance of granted funds to Operation Unit, Investigation Unit, and SP PHQ Garden. The role involves overseeing the Shaheed Compensation Package and providing financial assistance to eligible individuals. Additionally, the Officer manages other financial packages for the families of deceased officers, as well as specific packages for serving and retiring police officers and officials. This position demands a thorough understanding of financial regulations, compassionate handling of compensation matters, and efficient financial management within the police department.

  • HC Muhammad Ahmed

    Technical Assistance Unit (TAU)

    As the Technical Assistance Unit (TAU) & Operations Room Officer at DIGP South Zone Karachi, entrusted with the crucial responsibility of overseeing CCTV camera monitoring in high-security areas such as the Red Zone, during religious events, and protests, role extends to leading a dedicated unit focused on the recovery of snatched and stolen mobile phones, utilizing CCTV footages to identify and facilitate the arrest of criminals. Additionally, pivotal role in responding to Madadgar 15 police emergency calls, ensuring prompt and coordinated assistance. Actively engaging with citizen feedback.

  • HC Muhammad Zubair Khan

    PRO to DIGP South Zone

    As the PRO to DIGP South Zone & Media Cell Officer. I hold a central role in disseminating information and maintaining a positive public image for the department, responsibilities include the timely publication of news, ensuring effective communication with all media houses, and actively managing all social media platforms associated with DIGP South Zone. provides comprehensive photo coverage of all events, ensuring that the public is well-informed and engaged with the activities and initiatives of the South Zone Police Department. Through strategic communication and multimedia efforts contribute to fostering transparency, public trust, and community engagement.

  • L/ASI Faiza Jabbar

    Women & Children Protection Cell

    The Women & Children Protection Cell at DIGP South Zone Karachi is focused addressing a range of critical issues affecting the vulnerable segments of our community, dedicated to resolving complaints related to domestic violence, sexual assault, child abuses, child neglect, stalking, human trafficking, kidnapping of children, forced marriage, harassment at the workplace, kidnapping and abduction of women, and child marriage. Through a combination of compassionate intervention, legal advocacy, and collaborative efforts with relevant authorities and support services, creating a safer environment for women and children in the South Zone, further instrumental in providing protection, support, and justice to those affected by these sensitive issues.
